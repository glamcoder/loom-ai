# Loom Market Positioning

Loom is a working name.

## Short positioning

Loom is Make for AI workflows.

## Longer positioning

Loom is a Git-native workflow language for packaging reusable AI work as code. It compiles parameterized prompts and workflow definitions into agent-ready artifacts, deterministic traces, and eventually black-box LLM executions.

## What Loom is not

Loom is not a BAML clone, DSPy clone, LangGraph replacement, promptfoo replacement, Flowise replacement, or hosted agent platform in v0.

## Wedge

The v0 wedge is deterministic workflow packaging and compile-to-agent artifacts.

## Launch message

Stop copy-pasting prompts. Ship AI workflows as code.
